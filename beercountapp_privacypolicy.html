Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled in the BeerCount application.

1. No Personal Data Collected

BeerCount does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works entirely offline and does not require internet access.

2. Data Stored on Device

All data related to your beer count is stored locally on your device. It is not shared with any third parties.

3. Permissions

The app may request permission to access vibration functionality, which is used solely for user feedback when pressing the +1 button. No other sensitive permissions are required.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. No content is specifically directed toward children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with the updated date.
